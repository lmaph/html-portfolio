The Best Movies According to Lehlogonolo

My top 5 films of all-time

Parasite

Fantastic thriller filled with commentary about class.

The Godfather

Awesome crime drama filled with:
- Family values.
- Gorgeous cinematography.
- Great acting performances.
- Sweeping music score.
- Chronicles of the immigrant story.

Before Sunrise

Cosy and well-constructed romance film.

Enemy of the State

Gripping political thriller by Tony Scott... very well made.

A Goofy Movie

Warm-hearted Disney animated film for the family... amazing vibes & soundtrack.
